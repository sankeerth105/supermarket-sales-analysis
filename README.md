# supermarket-sales-analysis
A data analysis project on supermarket sales using Excel, focusing on revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior through interactive dashboards.
## Project Overview
This project analyzes supermarket sales data to uncover insights into revenue patterns, customer behavior, and product performance. The analysis is conducted using Excel with a focus on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and interactive dashboard creation.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and revenue trends
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate sales performance across branches and cities
- Discover peak sales periods and high-demand products

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Visualizations
- Dashboard Creation
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Power Query

## Key Performance Indicators(KPIs)
- Total Revenue
- Total Sales Quantity
- Average Transaction Value
- Gross Income
- Sales by Product Line
- Sales by City / Branch

## Key Insights
- Food & Beverages and Electronic Accessories drive the bulk of sales.
- Female customers lean more toward Fashion and Health & Beauty, while Male customers contribute more to Electronics and Sports/Travel.
- ₹15,379 → Profit margin is modest (~4.7%), suggesting tight competition or high operating costs.
- E-wallets are popular among both genders, showing digital adoption.
- Credit Cards are slightly more used by males, while Cash is still significant, especially among females.
- Membership programs are effective in driving repeat purchases and higher spending.
- Sales fluctuate across 2019, with noticeable peaks (likely seasonal promotions or holidays).
- Female customers show steadier contribution, while Male customers spike during certain months (possibly linked to electronics or travel-related purchases).
-  Yangon is the core market, but Mandalay/Naypyitaw could be growth opportunities with targeted campaigns.
